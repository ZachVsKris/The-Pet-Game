# Data sources and validation policy

## Names
- American Kennel Club, 2025 popular dog names
- Scope shown in game: AKC registrations and Canine Partners enrollments
- Male and female rankings are never compared with each other

## Breeds
- American Kennel Club dog-breed registration rankings
- Cat Fanciers' Association pedigreed-cat registration rankings
- These are registration rankings, not complete population censuses

## Companion-pet ownership
- UK Pet Food national pet-population survey
- Animal Medicines Australia, Pets in Australia national survey
- Selected Canadian national survey figures for prototype comparisons

## Inclusion rules
- Companion pets only
- No livestock or production animals
- Compare the same metric whenever possible
- Country questions use household ownership rates, not raw national totals
- Grouped categories such as “small mammals” are labeled exactly as grouped by the source

## Launch checklist
- Confirm each value and year against the linked source
- Confirm compatible definitions before any cross-country comparison
- Remove any question whose methodologies are materially inconsistent
- Review source terms before commercial publication
